üîë Key Differences from Traditional Model Hiring

  • Licensing Model: Customers buy rights to use face/likeness, not hiring time
  • Usage-Based Pricing: Price based on usage scope (online/broadcast/print) and duration
  • Territory Restrictions: Can limit usage to specific regions
  • Automated Delivery: AI generates video immediately after license purchase
  • Scalable: One model can have unlimited licenses simultaneously
  • Clear Terms: Each license has specific usage rights and expiration

üìã Typical Customer Journey

  1. Browse Models: Customer views modelProfiles on showcase page
  2. Select Model & Plan: Choose model and appropriate licensing plan
  3. Create Project: Input video specifications and scenario
  4. Generate Video: AI creates video using model's likeness
  5. Purchase License: Pay for chosen licensing plan
  6. Receive License: Get license certificate with usage rights
  7. Download Video: Access final video without watermarks
  8. Leave Review: Rate experience after using in campaign
